orbem formate
otherly > scott van kampen wieling

An observation from the outside that protects the inside.Thousands of years ago, if a Roman garrison found itself surrounded by an attacking enemy, but were required to hold their position on the field, their commander would call out the order, “orbem formate!”  orbem formate is an project that shows an Observation of the surrounding translated into a visual connection of materials that protects the core. Within this there is been looked towards the protection and caring side of plants and my surrounding and this made me question why I don’t have a prickly skin. An extra layer in between, to protect myself from the outside and to build a more trust on what is there in the inside, the core.

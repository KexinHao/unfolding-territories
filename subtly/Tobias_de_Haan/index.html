subtly > Tobias de Haan

over the last years, i noticed that we are losing connection
with each other and what is around us

our pace is increasing and accessibility is changing,
because we are constantly connected digitally
giving us many possibilities to choose from, at every moment

i want to appreciate the simple things again, re-connect,
find beauty in what already exists and always will be,
forever and everywhere.

Tobias de Haan

Tobias Reinbrandt de Haan (b.1993) is a visual artist based in The Hague, NL, and currently studies Documentary Photography at the Royal Academy of Art (KABK). His practice encompasses photographic, video, text and installation work, which together, are focussed on the dynamics between individuals, social structures, and the relationship between humans and nature. By paying attention to how people or cultural groups behave and interact with each other and their surroundings, he finds and depicts similarities and differences. During his projects, he works pro-active and hands-on, as he goes out to interact with its subjects. The work is meant to (re)-connect and build bridges between people, cultures and nature.Â

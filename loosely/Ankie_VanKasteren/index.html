loosely > Ankie van Kasteren

A project about hyper fixation as a tool to escape reality and stress of the daily life. The series consists of digital imagery, printed on satin paper and photo transfers on wood and mushroom paper.  I hope through taking the viewer on a journey, I’ll demonstrate the feeling of escaping in the woods. Hiding behind mycelium, mushroom paper and other visual experimentations as a social security blanket. This project kept me going, I fell in love with mushrooms. I find systems, logic and an amount of certainty in those uncertain times of 202122. I hope to inspire people to find their own coping strategies, and applaud them to use unconventional sustainable materials and discover the world around them with a different vision.   I learned lot about myself working with mushrooms, it was a fascinating ride and I will keep going for walks in the forest to explore more and try more ways of showing and representing them.
